What is the Farm Business Resilience Program

The Farm Business Resilience Program (FBRP) empowers farmers and graziers to improve their business resilience. The FBRP focuses on improving Economic, Social and Environmental resilience, via Farm Business Resilience Plans, workshops and training sessions, and on-farm demonstrations of practices which build resilience.

Farm Business Resilience Plans

Develop your Farm Business Resilience Plan to:
  • Support productivity and profitability.
  • Identify and assess risks (think droughts, natural disasters, and production uncertainties).
  • Chart strategies to manage risks and seize opportunities.

A written plan will also enable primary producers to apply for drought preparedness grants and subsidised loans aimed at improving resilience through the Queensland Rural and Industry Development Authority (QRIDA).

Need to get your plan on paper?
